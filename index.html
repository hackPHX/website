What is #hackPHX?

Hackathons rock, but the valley is sorely lacking — hackPHX wants that changed. We’re bringing the fun and camaraderie of day-long hackathons to the Phoenix area, starting with December 1st’s HTML5 Game Hackathon.

What is a hackathon?

Hackathons are events where people hack towards a specific goal. Hackathons focus on everything from local government, to a certain genre of applications to the particular framework used to create the applications. Hackathon participants have anywhere from 12 to 48 hours to build something awesome, whether as individuals or in teams, and show it off at the end the event.
