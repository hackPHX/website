About Us

hackPHX is a hackathon organization focused on bringing together amazing talent of both hardware and software engineers, designers and entrepreneurs in The Valley for competitive fun and games.

This organization’s goal is to bring together people of all ages within the community that are excited about hardware and/or software and want to get connected with others for a competitive outlet to show off their talents. Our target audience isn’t not just for college students, we’re interested in bringing together professionals and hobbyist as well.
